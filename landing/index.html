CPP (cents per point) measures how much value you extract from each loyalty point. A typical cash-back redemption gets you 1 CPP. Through the right transfer paths, the same points can be worth 2-5 CPP. The difference is real money: 100,000 points at 1 CPP = $1,000. At 2.5 CPP = $2,500.
